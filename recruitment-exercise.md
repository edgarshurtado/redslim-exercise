# Recruitment Exercise: Dataset Explorer MVP

Build a tool that helps users **discover, explore, and understand** the datasets in:

- `some_dataset_csv`
- `unistar_dk`

Aim for a **thoughtful MVP**, not a polished product. Use your imagination and choose the technology you think fits best.

## What we expect

- A working MVP in a **GitHub repository**
- A solution that shows both the app and **how it was built**
- **Meaningful git history** that shows your thinking, iteration, and trade-offs
- Basic engineering discipline

## Important note

Please **do not hand-code this manually**. Use modern tooling and AI-assisted development.

## What we want to learn

This exercise will be discussed in the interview:

- How you approach a blank page
- What technology and methods you choose, and why
- How you scope an MVP
- What challenges you hit and how you handled them
- How you practice agent-assisted engineering

Make it practical, understandable, and a bit fun.
